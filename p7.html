Artist Statement

Overall, the assignment was pretty simple and it was fun experimenting with different materials and render settings. Starting with the demo teapot, the tutorial was pretty easy to follow. I didn't really have any challenges making the teapot, but I was slighlty annoyed that whenever I wanted to create a new sketch, after I clicked on the plane desired for the sketch, Fusion would automatically flip the user orientaion, so I would constantly need fix the orientation with the cube in the upper right corner. Moving onto the personal teapot, I based the sketch off of my narwhal mug, but changed the horn into a pouring spout and added a blowhole lid. In terms of challenges, I couldn't figure out how to create the tail at the end of the handle, leading to me removing the tailfin in the final model. Fusion would keep giving me error messages that the lofts would intersect with the main body of the teapot even though I drew in rails and set the loft as new body.
